Pasta Red Pesto

Preparation Cook Spicy Serves
45 min 60 min Not spicy 4
Preparation Cook
45 min 60 min
Spicy Serves
Not spicy 4

Ingredients

800g Penne Rigate
185g Homemade or storebought Red Pesto
4 tbsp Olive Oil
2 Cloves of Garlic
75g Rocket Salad, for garnish
Parmigiano Reggiano
Large pinch of Salt
Black Pepper

Pasta Red Pesto

  1. Bring a large pan with water to the boil. Add a large pinch of salt to the water. It should taste a little salty. Add the penne rigate and cook till almost al dente.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan. Add the garlic and stir frequently till it become fragant. Now add the pesto and stir well.
  3. Add some large spoons of the pasta water to the pesto mixture. Keep on stirring till the water merges with the pesto. Let it thicken a little.
  4. Add the almost al dente penne to the pesto mixtureand cook for another minute. The be al dente and fully covered with pesto.
  5. Take of the heat. Now add a shit load of parmiganio reggiano and stir the pasta till it melts.
  6. Put the Red Pasta Pesto on a plate and top it off with some extra Parmiganio reggiano, rocket salad and black pepper. Enjoy!
