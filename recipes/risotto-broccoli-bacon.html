Risotto with Broccoli and Bacon

Preparation Cook Spicy Serves
45 min 60 min Not spicy 4
Preparation Cook
45 min 60 min
Spicy Serves
Not spicy 4

Ingredients

500g Italian Risotto Rice
1 Broccoli
1 Yellow Union
250g Bacon
100g Butter
200ml White Wine
800ml Chicken or Vegatable Broth (you might not need all of it!)
1 tbsp Extra Vergine Olive Oil
Parmigiano Reggiano
Black Pepper
Salt

Risotto with Broccoli and Bacon

  1. Cut the broccoli in bitsize pieces. Cook the broccoli in a small pan till just cooked. Set aside.
  2. Fry the bacon in a separate pan till brown and crisp. Remove from pan.
  3. Add olive oil, 50g butter and the remaining oil of the bacon to a large frying pan. Fry the onion on high heat till it becomes translucent and slightly brown. Turn down to medium heat. Add risotto rice and stir till it becomes translucent.
  4. Now add the white wine and gently keep stirring till all liquid has been absorbed by the rice. Keep adding small amounts of broth. When the liquid is absorbed you add another small amount of broth. The risotto rice is cooked when it is soft from the outside and still has a bite on the inside. You might not need all broth to get to that result.
  5. Add the bacon and brocolli when the rice is nearly cooked. Stir for another minute or two.
  6. Turn down the heat. Add 50 gram of butter and a shitload of grated Parmigiano Reggiano. Stir well till you risotto becomes super creamy. Add black pepper and salt to taste.
  7. Your risotto is ready to be plated. Buon appetito!
