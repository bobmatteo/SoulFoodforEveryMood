Butter Chicken

Preparation Cook Spicy Serves
45 min 60 min Not spicy 4
Preparation Cook
45 min 60 min
Spicy Serves
Not spicy 4

Ingredients for the Chicken Marinade

500g Chicken Thighs, cut in pieces
4 tbsp Greek Yoghurt
1 tsp Salt
1 tsp Kashmiri Chili Powder (available at Oriental)
1/2 tsp Massala (I use the brand H. Nandan)
1 tsp Garam Massala
1/2 tsp Ground Cumin Seeds
1/2 tsp Ground Coriander Seeds
Juice of a quarter lemon

Chicken marinate

  1. Add the chicken thighs, spices, yoghurt and lemon juice in a bowl
  2. Cover the bowl. Let the chicken marinate in the fridge for at least 2 hours (overnight would be even better!)

Ingredients for the Butter Chicken base

125g Butter
2 Big Yellow Onions
2 Cloves of Garlic
1 tsp Fresh Ginger
3 Tomatoes, cut in pieces
3 Bay Leaves
3 Cloves
2 Green Cardamon Pods
1 tsp Cumin Seeds
1 tbsp Tomato Puree
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Turmeric
1/2 tsp Kashmiri Chili Powder
1 tsp Ground Cinnamon
1/2 tsp Brown Sugar
1/2 tsp Garam Massala
200ml Double Cream
Dried Fenegriek Leaves, optional (available at Oriental)
Black Pepper, to taste
Cilantro
Tip! Recipe is paring well with naan (available at Oriental)

Butter Chicken

  1. Melt the butter in a big pan on medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds to the butter and fry until it get fragrant.
  3. Now add the onion, garlic and gember. Fry it for approx. 5 minutes. Take the mixture out of the pan and blend it in a blender. Make sure to leave the butter in the pan. Set aside.
  4. Add the tomatoes to the pan. Fry for approx. 3 minutes. Now take out the tomatoes and blend this to a smooth mixture (separate from the onion mixture).
  5. Shortly fry the bay leaves, cloves and cardamon pods to the remaining butter, till the spices becomes fragrant.
  6. Time to add the marinated chicken. Fry the chicken for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the onion mixture and stir well. Followed by the tomato mixture and tomato puree.
  8. Now, add the turmeric, Kashmiri chili powder, cinnamon and sugar.
  9. Let the chicken simmer for 20 minutes.
  10. After 20 minutes, it is time to add the double cream. Let is simmer for another 10 minutes.
  11. Add the garam massala, fenegriek leaves and black pepper to the butter chicken.
  12. Top it all off with cilantro. Serve the butter chicken with naan or white rice.
